AI DebaterX

DebaterX is a project that allows users to engage in a debate with a computer. The project utilizes web scraping and sentiment analysis to generate arguments and counterarguments for the user and the computer to debate on a given topic. The goal of DebaterX is to provide an engaging and informative experience for users who are interested in understanding different perspectives on a given issue. The sentiment analysis model allows the computer to analyze and understand the user's arguments and respond with appropriate counterarguments. The web scraping component allows the computer to gather relevant information and data to support its arguments. This project aims to provide a unique and interactive way for users to learn about different viewpoints and gain a deeper understanding of a given topic.

PDFARE

PDFARE is a web-based platform designed to help students easily share and access PDF documents. The platform offers a variety of features to support the sharing and organization of PDF documents. Users can upload and share their PDFs on the platform, and easily search and access documents shared by other users. The platform also allows users to organize their PDFs into custom folders, making it easy to find and access specific documents. In addition to sharing and organization, PDFARE also includes a built-in PDF viewer, allowing users to view and annotate documents without the need to download them. The platform is built with privacy and security in mind, and all the files are encrypted to protect the user's data. PDFARE is a convenient tool for students to share and access study materials, notes and other academic resources, it's also a great way to collaborate with classmates and to get access to a wide variety of study materials from different sources.
